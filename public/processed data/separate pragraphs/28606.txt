The Vice President of RotorWay International agreed to issue an Advisory Bulletin to all registered owners of RotorWay helicopters having the RI-162 engine informing them of the background of this accident as well as alternate way of carburetor icing prevention and avoidance . The pilot stated that the helicopter had some forward velocity on landing causing one of the skids to sink and get caught in the soft terrain .
