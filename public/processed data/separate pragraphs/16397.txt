During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on September 8 the airplane/lodge owner reported that while in level cruise flight the accident pilot noted a loud bang as the engine began to run rough and lose power . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors . The flight originated about 1115 from the airplane owner's remote lodge located about 100 miles southeast of Cordova  and was en route to Cordova .
