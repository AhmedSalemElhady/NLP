The round-robin flight was scheduled to transport personnel and cargo to 3 intermediate airstrips (Nuevamente Cashaschanes and Sumacal) servicing ranches also owned and operated by the owner of the airplane . During the turn the left wing of the airplane collided with the trunk of a 35-foot tall tree located approximately 100 feet west of the extended centerline for runway 15 . The business flight originated from Trinidad at 1245 local .
