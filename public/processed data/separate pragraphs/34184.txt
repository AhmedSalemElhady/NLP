The aft bearing (gear case cover) of the FCU/oil pump drive spur gear had disintegrated due to failure of the ribbon-type ball separator which was found in the bottom of the gearbox in two pieces and deformed . Compliance was due at the next accessory gearbox repair when the gearbox was opened . Postcrash examination of the engine revealed that the turbine had experienced oil starvation due to a failure of the oil pump .
