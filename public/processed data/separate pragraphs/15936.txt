On September 1 2001 at 1645 central daylight time an experimental Spears RV-6 N910RS was destroyed by impact forces and subsequent ground fire about 1 mile west of Campbell Airport (C81) Grayslake Illinois . A witness reported the airplane was flying at about 500 feet above ground level (agl) and was circling towards the airport when the airplane "barreled" left recover and then the nose of the airplane went straight down .
