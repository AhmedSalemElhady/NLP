1. On March 7 2000 at 1020 hours Pacific standard time a Bell 47G-4A N1354X made an uncommanded hard landing after takeoff from Wasco California . The aircraft sustained substantial damage; however the commercial pilot the sole occupant was not inured . The aircraft was being operated as an agricultural application flight by Inland Crop Dusters Inc under 14 CFR Part 137 when the accident occurred .
