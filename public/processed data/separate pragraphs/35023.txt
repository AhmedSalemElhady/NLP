The private pilot who was also a mechanic with an inspection authorization was landing the airplane from a ferry flight after the airplane had been involved in a previous accident (see ANC99LA155) . A doubler had been installed on the previously damaged firewall to provide mounting structure for the nose gear assembly . Inspection by an FAA airworthiness inspector revealed that a nose landing gear assembly from an earlier model Cessna 182 had been installed without a shimmy damper and without provision for connection to the steering bungees .
