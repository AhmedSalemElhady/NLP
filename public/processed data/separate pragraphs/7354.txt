On August 29 2000 about 0932 hours local a U S manufactured Sikorsky S55B helicopter New Zealand registry ZK-HSC operated by BA & JC Emeny Ltd collided with trees then terrain near Wanganui New Zealand . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . The helicopter was destroyed by impact and a postcrash fire and the commercial-rated pilot the sole occupant was fatally injured .
