On May 16 2001 at 1939 central daylight time a Kirchner Pietenpol Aircamper home built airplane N25157 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Tulsa Oklahoma . Examination of the airplane by FAA inspectors who responded to the accident site revealed that the landing gear had collapsed and partially separated .
