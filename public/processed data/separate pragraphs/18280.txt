On November 7 2001 approximately 2000 central standard time a Taylorcraft F19 N20002 piloted by a private pilot was substantially damaged during cruise flight when it struck a tree and subsequently impacted into a wooded area 9 miles west of Backus Minnesota . An examination of the engine and other airplane systems revealed no anomalies . The airplane's right wing was found in a tree approximately 120 feet prior to the airplane main wreckage .
