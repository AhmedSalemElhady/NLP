The air/oil separator hose to the scavenge pump was plugged about 10 inches along its length with a substance with a high lead content resulting in the recurring pilot report of excessive oil consumption . Examination of the engine revealed 18 quarts of oil were drained and 3 of the 6 connecting rods were failed due to lack of lubrication . During cruise flight at 14000 feet mean sea level the engine failed .
