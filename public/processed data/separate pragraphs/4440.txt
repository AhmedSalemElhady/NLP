The surviving rear seat passenger/photographer (pax) stated that during a preflight briefing the pilot indicated that they would fly by a cottage at reduced airspeed and at about 500 feet agl then they would climb to a higher altitude for more photographs . According to icing probability charts weather conditions were conducive for the formation of carburetor ice . The pax stated that after they flew by the cottage he did not hear an application of power to climb as expected .
