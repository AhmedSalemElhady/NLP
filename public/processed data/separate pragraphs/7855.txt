On September 29 2000 at 1200 hours mountain standard time a Hughes TH-55A N331SD experienced a main rotor-tailboom strike at the termination of a practice autorotation at Phoenix-Deer Valley Municipal airport in Phoenix Arizona . The helicopter sustained substantial damage; however the certificated flight instructor and his airplane-rated student were not injured .
