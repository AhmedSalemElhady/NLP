On April 61995 about 1305 eastern daylight time a Cessna U206G N756CW registered to Andre J Dussault operating as a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight experienced a total loss of engine power in cruise flight and crashed in the vicinity of Nassau N P Bahamas during a forced landing . Any further information may be obtained from: Director of Civil Aviation P O Box N-975 Nassau N P Bahamas Telephone: (809) 327-7281 This report is for information purposes only and contains only information released by the Bahamian Government . The airplane was destroyed .
