On September 25 1997 approximately 0939 central daylight time Trans World Airlines flight 593 a McDonald Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) airplane N931TW was not damaged during a runway overrun at New Orleans International Airport (Moisant Field) New Orleans Louisiana . Visual meteorological conditions existed at the time and an instrument flight plan was on file for the flight . The five crewmembers and 130 passengers were not injured and there was no emergency evacuation .
