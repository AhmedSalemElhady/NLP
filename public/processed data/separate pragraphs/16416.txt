During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on September 8 the airplane/lodge owner reported that while in level cruise flight the accident pilot noted a loud bang as the engine began to run rough and lose power . The certificated commercial pilot received minor injuries and the eight passengers were not injured . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) business flight under Title 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred .
