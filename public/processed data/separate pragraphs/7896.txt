On September 29 2000 at 1200 hours mountain standard time a Hughes TH-55A N331SD experienced a main rotor-tailboom strike at the termination of a practice autorotation at Phoenix-Deer Valley Municipal airport in Phoenix Arizona . The helicopter was operated by the Maricopa County Sheriff's Office as a public-use instructional flight under 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred .
