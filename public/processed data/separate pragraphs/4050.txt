On May 21 2000 at 1530 mountain standard time a Cessna 182N airplane N92755 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Nogales State of Sonora in the Republic of Mexico . The airplane was owned by Travis Air LLC of Mesa Arizona and was being operated by Arizona Aviation Inc of Mesa Arizona .
