On April 9 2001 about 1208 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-46-500TP N262MM registered to LK Aero Corporation collided with trees shortly after takeoff from Vero Beach Municipal Airport Vero Beach Florida while on a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight to Daytona Beach Florida . The airplane continued to descend and was observed to collide with trees on the edge of a golf course followed by a fire erupting .
