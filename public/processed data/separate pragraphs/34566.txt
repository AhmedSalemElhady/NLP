The left engine's magnetos were rotated using an electric hand-held drill and the left magneto did not produce any spark and the right magneto produced a spark in three of its six distributor cap posts . The pilot switched the left engine's boost pump from low to high; however the left engine continued to surge while the airplane lost altitude . A caution statement states in bold print 'If the auxiliary fuel pump switches are placed in the HIGH position with the engine-driven fuel pump(s) operating normally total loss of engine power may occur' .
