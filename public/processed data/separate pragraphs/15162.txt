On August 19 2001 about 1300 Pacific daylight time a LET Blanic L-13 glider N82AS sustained substantial damage while attempting an emergency landing at a private airstrip near Enumclaw Washington . The glider is registered to Puget Sound Soaring Association Kent Washington and was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) personal/pleasure flight under the provisions of Title 14 CFR Part 91 . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plan was filed for the local flight .
