On April 14 2001 about 1830 eastern daylight time a Grumman American AA-5B N81189 registered to and operated by a private owner as a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight  collided with a fence during landing at a private field in Dade City Florida . The pilot further stated that during the landing he was not in the center of the landing strip and he was blown into a fence which lined the edge of the strip . The flight originated in St Petersburg Florida the same day about 1800 .
