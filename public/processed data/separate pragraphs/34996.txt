The private pilot who was also a mechanic with an inspection authorization was landing the airplane from a ferry flight after the airplane had been involved in a previous accident (see ANC99LA155) . A doubler had been installed on the previously damaged firewall to provide mounting structure for the nose gear assembly . A review of photographs taken at the first accident site revealed that substantial damage to the left wing left horizontal stabilizer and left elevator (including missing the outboard 4 inches of elevator and the left elevator tip counterweight) tail cone and empennage and lower engine cowling existed prior to the ferry flight .
