On December 18 2001 approximately 1045 mountain standard time a Piper PA-28-235 N8704W operated by Cherokee Flyers LLC of Grand Junction Colorado was substantially damaged when it collided with terrain during landing at Crested Butte Colorado . The fuselage was also buckled . The pilot said that after walking along the runway and locating the point of touch down he noted a skid mark made by the left wheel and he suspected the brake may have locked .
