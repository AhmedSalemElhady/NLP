On September 2 2001 about 1430 Alaska daylight time a tundra tire-equipped Cessna 185F airplane N177BM sustained substantial damage while taxiing for takeoff at the King Salmon Airport King Salmon Alaska . The right main landing gear strut collapsed near the axle attach point and the right main wheel and tire rolled away from the airplane .
