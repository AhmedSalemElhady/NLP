1. On September 7 2001 about 1145 Alaska daylight time a wheel-equipped de Havilland DHC-3 airplane N254AW sustained substantial damage during a forced landing about 53 miles east of Cordova Alaska . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) business flight under Title 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred . The airplane was registered to Thomas J Prijatel and operated by Alaska Wilderness Outfitting Company LLC Cordova Alaska .
