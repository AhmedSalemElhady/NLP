Prior to departure icing advisories were issued to the pilot for his route of flight; temperatures aloft ranging from -6 to -8 degrees C The pilot advised the passenger of icing conditions prior to departure and reassured her they could deviate if those conditions were encountered . The passenger reported that the flight was delayed a day due to weather and that she was scheduled to attend a class at the destination the day of the accident . One half-inch-thick pieces of ice matching the leading edges of the wings were observed in the vicinity of the wreckage .
