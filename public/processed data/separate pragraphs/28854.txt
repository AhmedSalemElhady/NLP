While the solo student pilot of the Cessna 152 was on downwind leg for landing the air traffic controller directed the student's attention to a Boeing 757 aircraft that was preceding the student's aircraft for landing on the parallel runway . The report concluded that the Cessna 152 descended into the Boeing 757's wake when the wake was 37 to 41 seconds old . The student's flight instructor told the Safety Board that the student who had 136 hours of dual instruction and 20 hours of solo flight time had received instruction in wake turbulence avoidance at three points in ground instruction .
