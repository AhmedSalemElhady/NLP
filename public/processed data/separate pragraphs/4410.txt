The surviving rear seat passenger/photographer (pax) stated that during a preflight briefing the pilot indicated that they would fly by a cottage at reduced airspeed and at about 500 feet agl then they would climb to a higher altitude for more photographs . The airplane impacted trees and fell to the ground . The pax noted their low altitude over the mountainous/hilly terrain and 'yelled' for the pilot to climb to avoid trees .
