According to personnel at the Portland air traffic control tower Horizon Air and the FAA the aircraft was on an approximate four mile final to runway 10R when the crew noticed an increasing turbine inlet temperature indication on the number two engine a vibration and a burning odor within the aircraft's cabin . The pilot declared an emergency and during the rollout the engine fire warning indicator illuminated and the engine was shut down . The crew and passengers then evacuated through the forward left main cabin exit onto the runway .
