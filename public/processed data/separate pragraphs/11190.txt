On March 28 2001 about 1300 Alaska standard time a wheel/ski equipped Cessna 180 airplane N91303 sustained substantial damage while landing at an off airport site located about 60 miles west of Iliamna Alaska . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on April 11 the pilot reported that he was landing to the northeast on a frozen slough .
