0. On August 11 2001 approximately 1100 central daylight time a Piper PA-28-235 airplane N235RM was substantially damaged when it made a hard landing at the Dallas Airpark East near Terrell Texas . According to the pilot he was landing at Dallas Airpark East and touched down "hard short of the runway" . The pilot then flew to Redbird Airport where the airplane was then later examined by an FAA inspector and a mechanic .
