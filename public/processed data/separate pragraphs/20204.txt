On December 28 2001 at 1428 central standard time a Boeing 737-522 N941UA operated by United Airlines (UAL) as Flight 1260 declared an emergency due to a reported control system problem during decent to the Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD) Chicago Illinois . Following the vibrations the airplane began an uncommanded roll to the right for approximately 20 degrees .
