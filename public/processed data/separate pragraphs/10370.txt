On February 15 2001 about 1540 hours Pacific standard time a midair collision occurred between a Cessna 152 N68763 and a Cessna 172N N4686G over the Pacific Ocean about 6 nautical miles south of the Long Beach Municipal Airport Long Beach California . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plans were filed .
