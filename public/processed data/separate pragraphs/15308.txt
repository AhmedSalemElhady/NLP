On August 22 2001 about 2100 Alaska daylight time a tundra tire equipped Piper PA-12 airplane N7604H sustained substantial damage during landing at an off airport site about 18 miles east of Nenana Alaska . During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on August 23 the pilot reported that he was landing to the west on a 800 feet long site . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors .
