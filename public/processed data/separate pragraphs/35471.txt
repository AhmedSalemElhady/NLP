On September 13 1999 about 2045 Universal Coordinated Time a Kaman Aerospace K1200 K-MAX helicopter registration D-HFZA operated by Heli Air Zagel of Hersbruck Germany impacted terrain during a forced landing in Austria and was destroyed . The forced landing was reportedly precipitated by a loss of engine power during a logging operation . The investigation is under the jurisdiction of the Austrian government .
