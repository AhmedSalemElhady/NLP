On November 22 2001 about 1330 central standard time a Enstrom 280FX N280XF registered to Tread-Aire LLC collided with electrical wires while approaching runway 13 at Reeves Airport Tallassee Alabama while on a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed at the time and no flight plan was filed . The helicopter was destroyed and the commercial-rated pilot received serious injuries .
