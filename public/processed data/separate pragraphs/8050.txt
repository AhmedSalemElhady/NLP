On September 29 2000 at 1200 hours mountain standard time a Hughes TH-55A N331SD experienced a main rotor-tailboom strike at the termination of a practice autorotation at Phoenix-Deer Valley Municipal airport in Phoenix Arizona . The flight instructor reported that he was demonstrating a 180-degree practice autorotation in the north traffic pattern . The flight instructor stated that he did not perceive any action or inaction on his part that could be construed as detrimental to the safe outcome of the maneuver .
