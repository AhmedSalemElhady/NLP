Postcrash examination of the wreckage revealed the left wing leading edge was found crushed aft to the spar at the midpoint and the left wing spray boom was dented/bent approximately 25 feet inboard from the outboard end . The airplane flew over a row of 30 to 40 foot high trees and impacted the ground with the right wing low . Another witness reported that he saw the airplane coming over the trees with 'something hanging down under the left wing at about the midpoint (like a support brace)' .
