Witnesses reported that the airplane lifted off the runway accelerated as it remained in ground effect and near the departure end of the runway climbed in a vertical attitude banked and then descended . The flight instructor had 171 hours in this low wing single engine aircraft including one night flight (15 hours) with takeoffs and landings .
