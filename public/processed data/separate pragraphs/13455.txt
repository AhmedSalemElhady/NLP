On June 23 2001 at 1158 central daylight time a Cessna 182Q N4834N was substantially damaged when it impacted terrain following a loss of engine power during the initial takeoff climb near Houston Texas . The airplane was registered to Southwestern Gas Pipeline Inc of The Woodlands Texas and operated by the pilot . According to witnesses the airplane departed from runway 17L and was climbing through 300 feet when the engine "failed" .
