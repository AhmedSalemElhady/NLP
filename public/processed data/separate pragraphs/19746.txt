On December 18 2001 approximately 1045 mountain standard time a Piper PA-28-235 N8704W operated by Cherokee Flyers LLC of Grand Junction Colorado was substantially damaged when it collided with terrain during landing at Crested Butte Colorado . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plan had been filed for the personal flight being conducted under Title 14 CFR Part 91 .
