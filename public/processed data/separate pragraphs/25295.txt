On September 5 1997 about 0940 Atlantic daylight time a Cessna A188B 9YTIQ collided with a house after departing Camden Field Couva Trinidad W I The airplane was operated by Briko Air Services Ltd . According to the government of Trinidad and Tobago the airplane departed from Camden Field in Couva Trinidad which is approximately 10 miles south of Piarco International Airport .
