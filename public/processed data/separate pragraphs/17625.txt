On October 24 2001 at 0315 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-28-181 N4149D collided with trees following a loss of engine power about four miles west of the Brunswick; Glynco Jetport in Brunswick Georgia . The flight had originated in Nashville Tennessee at 2330 the previous day . According to the pilot while enroute to Orlando Florida he noticed that he was low on fuel and contacted "flight control" for directions to the nearest airport .
