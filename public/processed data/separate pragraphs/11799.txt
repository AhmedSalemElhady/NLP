On April 14 2001 about 1830 eastern daylight time a Grumman American AA-5B N81189 registered to and operated by a private owner as a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight  collided with a fence during landing at a private field in Dade City Florida . He stated that he tried to add power and execute a go-around but after the aircraft collided with the fence he committed himself to landing . The pilot stated that he was performing a soft field landing at a private grass strip on about a 260-degree heading with a right crosswind .
