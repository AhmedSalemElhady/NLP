The aft bearing (gear case cover) of the FCU/oil pump drive spur gear had disintegrated due to failure of the ribbon-type ball separator which was found in the bottom of the gearbox in two pieces and deformed . Postcrash examination of the engine revealed that the turbine had experienced oil starvation due to a failure of the oil pump . The mechanic reported that he had replaced the number 25 bearing and the spur adapter gear shaft 265 hours and 4 months before the accident which would have provided access to the gearbox to accomplish the bulletin .
