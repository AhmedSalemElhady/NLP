The Vice President of RotorWay International agreed to issue an Advisory Bulletin to all registered owners of RotorWay helicopters having the RI-162 engine informing them of the background of this accident as well as alternate way of carburetor icing prevention and avoidance . Post accident investigation revealed that the carburetor heat system was operating . The temperature and dewpoint on the surface was reported to be 42 degrees F and 38 degrees F respectively .
