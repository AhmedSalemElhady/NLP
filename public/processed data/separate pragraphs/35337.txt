On September 13 1999 about 2045 Universal Coordinated Time a Kaman Aerospace K1200 K-MAX helicopter registration D-HFZA operated by Heli Air Zagel of Hersbruck Germany impacted terrain during a forced landing in Austria and was destroyed . According to a representative of Kaman Aerospace the pilot reportedly stated that after a loss of engine power he jettisoned his external load and executed a forced landing . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors .
