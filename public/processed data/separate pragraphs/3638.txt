On May 10 2000 at 1500 hours Pacific daylight time a Sikorsky S61A N693CC experienced a dual loss of engine power on approach to an off-airport landing site and made a forced landing approximately 28 miles east of Eureka California . The pilot stated that the fuel filter light had not illuminated during the flight . The helicopter was being operated under the provisions of 14 CFR Part 133 as an external load operation .
