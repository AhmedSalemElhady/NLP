On February 15 2001 about 1540 hours Pacific standard time a midair collision occurred between a Cessna 152 N68763 and a Cessna 172N N4686G over the Pacific Ocean about 6 nautical miles south of the Long Beach Municipal Airport Long Beach California . Both airplanes were rented from and operated by the same flight school the Long Beach Flying Club & Flight Academy and both airplanes were destroyed .
