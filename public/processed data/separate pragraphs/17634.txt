On October 24 2001 at 0315 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-28-181 N4149D collided with trees following a loss of engine power about four miles west of the Brunswick; Glynco Jetport in Brunswick Georgia . The pilot said he was at 3000 feet mean sea level and nine miles from the airport when he lost engine power . Air traffic control gave him a heading towards the nearest airport which was Glynco Jetport .
