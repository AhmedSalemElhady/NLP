At V1 the DC-10-30's number 1 engine a General Electric CF6-50C2 experienced a casing breach when the 2nd-stage low pressure turbine (LPT) anti-rotation nozzle locks failed . In May 1993 the engine manufacturer issued a service bulletin to replace existing nozzle locks with ones that had thicker posts and arms .
