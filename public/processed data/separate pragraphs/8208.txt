On October 10 2000 about 2230 greenwich mean time (GMT) a McDonnell Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) N963TW operated as Trans World Airlines flight number 920 encountered in-flight pitch oscillations during the flight's descent prior to landing at Las Americas International Airport Santo Domingo Dominican Republic (SDQ) . An emergency was declared and an uneventful landing was made .
