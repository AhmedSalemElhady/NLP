On May 10 2000 at 1500 hours Pacific daylight time a Sikorsky S61A N693CC experienced a dual loss of engine power on approach to an off-airport landing site and made a forced landing approximately 28 miles east of Eureka California . The pilot reported that while performing logging operations the low fuel pressure light flickered briefly . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed .
