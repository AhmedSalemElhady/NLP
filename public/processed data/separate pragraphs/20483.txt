On January No 13 1994 about 1230 hours Greenwich Mean Time a Beech BE-90 N46WA registered to Charles Kuykendall Wilmer Texas was destroyed during a ditching in international waters about 50 nautical miles south of Martigues France . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . The German national commercial pilot the sole occupant received minor injuries .
