0. The airplane had received its last annual inspection on July 18 1997 26 months before the first accident . The FAA issued a ferry permit for the airplane but did not inspect the airplane nor verify the extent of damage which existed prior to the flight occurring . A review of photographs taken at the first accident site revealed that substantial damage to the left wing left horizontal stabilizer and left elevator (including missing the outboard 4 inches of elevator and the left elevator tip counterweight) tail cone and empennage and lower engine cowling existed prior to the ferry flight .
