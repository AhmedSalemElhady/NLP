0. On July 22 2001 about 1510 eastern daylight time a Beech BE-36 N3680U was substantially damaged during takeoff at the Biddeford Municipal Airport (B19) Biddeford Maine . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and a visual flight rules flight plan had been filed for the flight destined for the Westchester County Airport White Plains New York . The airplane was departing runway 24 a 3011 foot-long asphalt runway .
