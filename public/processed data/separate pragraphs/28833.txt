While the solo student pilot of the Cessna 152 was on downwind leg for landing the air traffic controller directed the student's attention to a Boeing 757 aircraft that was preceding the student's aircraft for landing on the parallel runway . The threshold of the parallel runway is abeam and 500 feet to the right of the runway the student was to land on . The controller then advised of a 10-knot quartering headwind from the right issued a wake turbulence precaution and cleared the student pilot for the landing option .
