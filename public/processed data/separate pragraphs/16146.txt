On September 2 2001 about 1430 Alaska daylight time a tundra tire-equipped Cessna 185F airplane N177BM sustained substantial damage while taxiing for takeoff at the King Salmon Airport King Salmon Alaska . The airplane was operated by Bill Martin Fish Alaska Inc King Salmon . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) cross-country business flight under Title 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred .
