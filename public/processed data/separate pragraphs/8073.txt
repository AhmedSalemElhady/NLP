After he and the student exited the helicopter he noted that the main rotor blades had contacted the tailboom damaging the tail rotor drive shaft tailboom as well as all the main rotor and tail rotor blades . The helicopter was operated by the Maricopa County Sheriff's Office as a public-use instructional flight under 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred .
