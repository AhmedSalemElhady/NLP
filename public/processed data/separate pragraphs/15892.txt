On September 1 2001 at 1645 central daylight time an experimental Spears RV-6 N910RS was destroyed by impact forces and subsequent ground fire about 1 mile west of Campbell Airport (C81) Grayslake Illinois . The 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight had originated from Roanoke Texas with C81 as the intended final destination .
