Witnesses reported that as the student pilot was on final approach descending through approximately 100-foot altitude (agl) the aircraft abruptly rolled inverted and crashed short of the runway threshold . The threshold of the parallel runway is abeam and 500 feet to the right of the runway the student was to land on . The student's flight instructor told the Safety Board that the student who had 136 hours of dual instruction and 20 hours of solo flight time had received instruction in wake turbulence avoidance at three points in ground instruction .
