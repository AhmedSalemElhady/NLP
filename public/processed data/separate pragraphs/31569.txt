On December 6 1998 about 0915 eastern standard time a Piper PA-38-112 N9471T registered to Bug Air Transport Co operating as a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight crashed into a salt marsh 6 miles west of Marsh Harbor International Airport Abaco Bahamas following a sudden engine stoppage . The airplane received substantial damage and the ATP-rated pilot the lone occupant was not injured .
