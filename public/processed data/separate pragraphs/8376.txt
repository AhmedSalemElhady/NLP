On October 26 2000 about 1105 hours Pacific daylight time a Grumman American AA-5A N26470 was destroyed when it made an off airport forced landing in Gardena California . Witnesses on the ground reported the engine sputtered a few times before it quit making any sounds and the propeller stopped turning .
