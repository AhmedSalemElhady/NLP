During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on September 8 the airplane/lodge owner reported that while in level cruise flight the accident pilot noted a loud bang as the engine began to run rough and lose power . During the subsequent forced landing in a marshy muskeg-covered area the airplane nosed over and sustained substantial damage to right wing strut engine firewall and empennage . He said that as the pilot was performing the engine emergency procedures he smelled smoke in the cabin and noted that the oil pressure was rapidly decreasing .
