On July 2 2000 about 2052 central daylight time a Gulfstream American AA5A N26937 registered to an individual made a forced landing in trees near Selma Alabama following loss of engine power while on a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight . The aircraft received substantial damage and the commercial-rated pilot and one passenger received minor injuries . After takeoff from Selma while climbing through 3500 feet he noticed the engine oil pressure was on zero .
