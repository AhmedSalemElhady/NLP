On December 11 2000 at 1445 local time (1845 GMT) a Cessna U206G single-engine airplane Bolivian registration CP-1721 was destroyed upon collision with a tree and terrain while landing at a private airstrip near Sumacal Department of Beni in the Republic of Bolivia . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors . The airplane was owned and operated by Servicio Aereo Manchupo of Trinidad Bolivia .
