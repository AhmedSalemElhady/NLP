On May 10 2000 at 1500 hours Pacific daylight time a Sikorsky S61A N693CC experienced a dual loss of engine power on approach to an off-airport landing site and made a forced landing approximately 28 miles east of Eureka California . The helicopter owned and operated by Croman Corporation White City Oregon sustained substantial damage .
