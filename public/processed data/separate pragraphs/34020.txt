The flight operating as AVIATECA Flight 901 (GU901) was on an IFR flight plan from La Aurora International Airport Guatamala City Guatemala to San Salvador International Airport San Salvador El Salvador . All of the 7 crewmembers and 58 passengers were fatally injured . The aircraft was being operated by AVIATECA SA the national airline of Guatamala which operates as a domestic and international airline .
