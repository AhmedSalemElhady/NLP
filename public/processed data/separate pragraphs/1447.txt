On January 22 2000 about 1430 hours Pacific standard time a Mince Jurka MJ77 N751JR sustained substantial damage during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Rio Vista California . The commercial pilot/owner operated the airplane under the provisions of 14 CFR Part 91 and was not injured . The personal flight departed Stockton California en route to Petaluma California about 1400 .
