The pilots reported that they noted evidence of carburetor ice during the flight; therefore as they began their descent to the destination they elected to use carburetor heat throughout the remainder of the flight . According to icing probability charts conditions were marginally conducive (borderline) for carburetor ice . However they were flying in conditions of snow and 23 miles south at Zanesville OH the temperature and dew point were reported to be 19 and 14 degrees F respectively .
