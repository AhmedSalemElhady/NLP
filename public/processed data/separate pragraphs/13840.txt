On June 29 2001 about 1350 Alaska daylight time a wheel-equipped Piper PA-18-150 airplane N6881B sustained substantial damage during a forced landing at a private airstrip located about 2 miles north of Palmer Alaska . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) personal flight under Title 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plan was filed .
