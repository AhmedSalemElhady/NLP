On December 11 2000 at 1445 local time (1845 GMT) a Cessna U206G single-engine airplane Bolivian registration CP-1721 was destroyed upon collision with a tree and terrain while landing at a private airstrip near Sumacal Department of Beni in the Republic of Bolivia . Avenida Mariscal Santa Cruz # 1278 Casilla Postal 9360 La Paz BoliviaThis report is for informational purposes only and contains only information released by or obtained for the Government of Bolivia . The round-robin flight was scheduled to transport personnel and cargo to 3 intermediate airstrips (Nuevamente Cashaschanes and Sumacal) servicing ranches also owned and operated by the owner of the airplane .
