The aft bearing (gear case cover) of the FCU/oil pump drive spur gear had disintegrated due to failure of the ribbon-type ball separator which was found in the bottom of the gearbox in two pieces and deformed . The balls were ejected into the gear case and one was ingested by the oil pump which caused internal jamming and the resultant shearing of the splined adapter which drives the oil pump . Allison CEB 1228 issued November 20 1985 calls for bearings with the ribbon-type pressed steel separator to be replaced with bearings that incorporate a one-piece machined steel separator .
