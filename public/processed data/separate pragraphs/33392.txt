According to witnesses working several miles east of the accident site they heard an airplane flying overhead traveling east to west and low to the ground; however low cloud cover snow and poor visibility prevented them from viewing the aircraft . The last National Track Analysis Program (NTAP) radar 'hit' of the accident airplane was at 1019:23 at an altitude of 12600 feet . The pilot was advised to re-contact Denver ARTCC for further advisories but failed to do so .
