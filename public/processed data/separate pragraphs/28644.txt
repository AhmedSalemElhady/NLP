The Vice President of RotorWay International agreed to issue an Advisory Bulletin to all registered owners of RotorWay helicopters having the RI-162 engine informing them of the background of this accident as well as alternate way of carburetor icing prevention and avoidance . The pilot stated that during the descent phase of flight the engine temperatures were indicating in the low end of the green arc but he did not notice the carburetor temperature gauge indication . The pilot stated that the helicopter had some forward velocity on landing causing one of the skids to sink and get caught in the soft terrain .
