The flightcrew reported that during the application of takeoff power when the throttles were in approximately the vertical position the numbers 1 and 3 engines were indicating approximately 70% N1 . Examination of the engine revealed that the low pressure turbine (LPT) stage one disk had separated from the LPT rotor at the stage one to two flange joint . Examination of several of the lug arms revealed 'noticeable wear patterns' and 'crack arrest positions indicative of fatigue cracking' .
