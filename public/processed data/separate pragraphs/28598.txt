The Vice President of RotorWay International agreed to issue an Advisory Bulletin to all registered owners of RotorWay helicopters having the RI-162 engine informing them of the background of this accident as well as alternate way of carburetor icing prevention and avoidance . The temperature and dewpoint on the surface was reported to be 42 degrees F and 38 degrees F respectively . According to the pilot/builder the homebuilt helicopter was in cruise at 1000 feet agl when he initiated a 200-300 foot per minute descent .
