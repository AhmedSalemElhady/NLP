On June 25 2001 about 1930 Alaska daylight time a float-equipped Piper PA-18-150 airplane N7050 sustained substantial damage when it collided with tundra-covered terrain about 80 miles north-northwest of King Salmon Alaska . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed in the area of the accident and VFR flight following procedures were in effect .
