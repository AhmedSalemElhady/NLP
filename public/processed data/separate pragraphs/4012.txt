On May 21 2000 at 1530 mountain standard time a Cessna 182N airplane N92755 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Nogales State of Sonora in the Republic of Mexico . During a forced landing in rough and uneven sandy terrain the nose landing gear collapsed resulting in a loss of control . The personal flight originated from Mulege State of Baja California Sur Mexico at 1230 .
