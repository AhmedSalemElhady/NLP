This report is filed for information purposes only and contains only information provided by the Nicaraguan aviation authorities . On January 29 1995 about 0811 central standard time N59556 a Bell 206B3 helicopter registered to Reeder Flying Service and operated by Helicopteros SA experienced a hard landing near Neacon Nicaragua while on an executive flight to a mining area .
