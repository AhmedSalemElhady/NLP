On December 26 2001 about 1900 eastern standard time a Cessna 182 N660DD experienced a total loss of engine power and was substantially damage during a forced landing near Falmouth Kentucky . The reported weather at an airport approximately 20 miles away from the accident site at 1851 was: wind from 270 degrees at 16 knots gusting to 20 knots; visibility 10 miles; ceiling 9000 feet overcast; temperature 25 degrees F; dew point 16 degrees F; altimeter 2981 inches hg .
