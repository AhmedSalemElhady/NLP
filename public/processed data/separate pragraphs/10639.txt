According to personnel at the Portland air traffic control tower Horizon Air and the FAA the aircraft was on an approximate four mile final to runway 10R when the crew noticed an increasing turbine inlet temperature indication on the number two engine a vibration and a burning odor within the aircraft's cabin . The PT120A engine has been removed from the aircraft and quarantined awaiting coordination with Pratt & Whitney for further examination and disassembly . The three crew and 30 passengers were uninjured .
