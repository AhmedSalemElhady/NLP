The flight initially touched down at 584 feet from the end of Runway 14 the left wing tip touched the pavement three separate times before the aircraft departed the runway at a 40 degree angle traveling through rough terrain and coming to rest approximately 1148 feet from the point where it exited the runway . The Digital Flight Data Recorder (DFDR) Study showed that while on approach the airplane rolled approximately 20 degrees to the right . The first officer (FO) was on the controls and had completed the VOR-DME approach which had been notamed 'out of service' at the time of the accident .
