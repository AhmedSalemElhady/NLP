0. On August 14 2000 about 1252 hours Pacific daylight time a Beech K35 N878R owned and operated by the pilot experienced an in-flight emergency during cruise flight . Thereafter according to witnesses the airplane cartwheeled sheered off its wings engine and propeller assembly and came to rest about 75 feet north of the highway . Oil was observed on components including the face (rearward surface) of the propeller blades the exposed portion of the crankshaft upper and lower engine cowl the front windscreen fuselage belly and the empennage .
