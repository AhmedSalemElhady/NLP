On January No 13 1994 about 1230 hours Greenwich Mean Time a Beech BE-90 N46WA registered to Charles Kuykendall Wilmer Texas was destroyed during a ditching in international waters about 50 nautical miles south of Martigues France . According to the French Bureau Enquets Accidents and the FAA the airplane was being ferried from Germany to Dallas Texas with a planned stop in Portugal .
