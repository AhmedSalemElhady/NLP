On March 18 1995 about 1321 eastern standard time a Convair 440 N137CA registered to Winemiller Aviation collided with a parked U S Army UH-60 Blackhawk helicopter while landing at Cap Haitien Haiti while on a 14 CFR Part 125 nonscheduled passenger flight . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed at the time and an instrument flight rules flight plan was filed . The Convair 440 and the UH-60 received substantial damage .
