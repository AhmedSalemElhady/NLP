The surviving rear seat passenger/photographer (pax) stated that during a preflight briefing the pilot indicated that they would fly by a cottage at reduced airspeed and at about 500 feet agl then they would climb to a higher altitude for more photographs . The pax said the pilot did not respond but was leaning forward as if to be trying to read or reach something . According to icing probability charts weather conditions were conducive for the formation of carburetor ice .
