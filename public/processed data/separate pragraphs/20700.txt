On January 29 1995 about 0811 central standard time N59556 a Bell 206B3 helicopter registered to Reeder Flying Service and operated by Helicopteros SA experienced a hard landing near Neacon Nicaragua while on an executive flight to a mining area . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plan was filed for the local flight . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed .
