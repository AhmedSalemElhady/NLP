On November 7 2001 approximately 2000 central standard time a Taylorcraft F19 N20002 piloted by a private pilot was substantially damaged during cruise flight when it struck a tree and subsequently impacted into a wooded area 9 miles west of Backus Minnesota . The airplane main wreckage was found resting on the ground on its right side cabin .
