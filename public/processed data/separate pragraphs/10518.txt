On December 14 1995 about 0014 central standard time N503U an Aero Commander 1121 operated by American Air Network crashed about 10 miles North of Guatemala City Guatemala while on a 14 CFR Part 135 on-demand international cargo flight . Instrument meteorological conditions prevailed at the time and an IFR flight plan was filed . The flight had originated from Miami Florida about 2200 the previous day .
