According to FAA Memphis Air Route Traffic Control transcripts the airplane radioed Approach Control radar at 1748 central daylight time requesting VFR flight following to the Holly Springs Airport . Air Force Rescue Coordination Center Langley picked up ELT transmissions south of the Holly Springs Airport at 2001 and alerted Mississippi Civil Air Patrol at 0300 . The aircraft wreckage was located at 0745 on July 15 2000 .
