On October 20 2001 at 1250 eastern daylight time a North American SNJ-6 N453WA collided on the ground with a Cessna CE-172-R N64AF after landing at Kissimmee Municipal Airport in Kissimmee Florida . Reportedly the Cessna landed on runway 6 and cleared the runway at taxiway Delta .
