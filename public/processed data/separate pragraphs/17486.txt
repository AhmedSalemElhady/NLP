On October 20 2001 at 1250 eastern daylight time a North American SNJ-6 N453WA collided on the ground with a Cessna CE-172-R N64AF after landing at Kissimmee Municipal Airport in Kissimmee Florida . The instructional flight of the Cessna was operated under the provisions of Title 14 CFR Part 91 with an instrument flight plan on file . After clearing the pilot of the Cessna contacted ground control and was advised to hold position .
