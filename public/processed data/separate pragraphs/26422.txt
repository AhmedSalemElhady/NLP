On April 61995 about 1305 eastern daylight time a Cessna U206G N756CW registered to Andre J Dussault operating as a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight experienced a total loss of engine power in cruise flight and crashed in the vicinity of Nassau N P Bahamas during a forced landing . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . The pilot stated he was in cruise flight at 1500 feet agl when he experienced a total loss of engine power .
