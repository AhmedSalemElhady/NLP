0. On June 25 1999 at 2130 central daylight time a Beech C-23 N9703Q piloted by a private pilot was destroyed during a collision with the ground following a loss of control in flight . One of the witnesses said she saw the airplane pitching up and down and rolling left and right violently before losing sight of it due to very heavy rains . N9703Q's wreckage trail went up a hill about 500 feet .
