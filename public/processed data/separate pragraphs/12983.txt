On May 27 2001 at 1255 hours Pacific daylight time a Cessna 337G N337GD sustained substantial damage when it impacted electrical power transmission wires about 5 miles south of Temecula California . The personal cross-country flight departed Brown Field San Diego California about 1235 and was destined for the French Valley Airport Temecula . According to a Federal Aviation Administration inspector the airplane impacted power transmission wires approximately 120 feet above ground level while over interstate highway 15 .
