On September 29 2000 at 1200 hours mountain standard time a Hughes TH-55A N331SD experienced a main rotor-tailboom strike at the termination of a practice autorotation at Phoenix-Deer Valley Municipal airport in Phoenix Arizona . The flight was originating from the north ramp at Deer Valley after a prior series of 10 practice autorotations at the time of the accident .
