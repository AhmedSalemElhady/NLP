On August 26 2001 at 0954 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-32-300 N2116G collided with power lines and a tree while executed an emergency off-airport landing in Homerville Georgia after experiencing a loss of engine power in flight . The pilot had switched tanks several times throughout the flight but within five minutes of the last tank switch the airplane experienced a loss of engine power .
