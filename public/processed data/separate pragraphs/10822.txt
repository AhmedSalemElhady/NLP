On March 6 2001 approximately 0740 Pacific Standard Time a De Havilland DHC-8-102 N822PH registered to First Security Bank NA and crewed by an airline transport pilot a commercial pilot and a flight attendant sustained minor damage during an in-flight fire warning event within the number two engine nacelle while on final approach to Portland International Airport Portland Oregon . The three crew and 30 passengers were uninjured . The crew and passengers then evacuated through the forward left main cabin exit onto the runway .
