On March 23 2001 about 1550 hours mountain standard time a Cessna 305A N5188G owned and operated by the front seated pilot ground looped during landing rollout on runway 04 at the Winslow-Lindbergh Regional Airport Winslow Arizona . The private pilot had just purchased the airplane and was in the process of flying it to his Annapolis Maryland home with the aid of the CFI . The instructional flight was performed under 14 CFR Part 91 and it originated from Needles California about 14 hours earlier .
