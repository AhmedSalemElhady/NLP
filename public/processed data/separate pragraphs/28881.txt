While the solo student pilot of the Cessna 152 was on downwind leg for landing the air traffic controller directed the student's attention to a Boeing 757 aircraft that was preceding the student's aircraft for landing on the parallel runway . The Safety Board prepared a Study of Wake Turbulence Encounter using data from the Boeing 757's flight data recorder tower communication tapes and tower reported surface winds . Witnesses reported that as the student pilot was on final approach descending through approximately 100-foot altitude (agl) the aircraft abruptly rolled inverted and crashed short of the runway threshold .
