According to preliminary information the Bell 205A-1 helicopter was at an out-of-ground effect hover over a pond near the Snake River using a long line and bucket to pick up water during fire fighting operations . The airline transport pilot of the Bell 205A-1 and the airline transport pilot of the Bell 212 were not injured . While the Bell 212 helicopter was operating in close proximity its long line and bucket got tangled in the Bell 205A-1's main rotor system .
