On April 61995 about 1305 eastern daylight time a Cessna U206G N756CW registered to Andre J Dussault operating as a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight experienced a total loss of engine power in cruise flight and crashed in the vicinity of Nassau N P Bahamas during a forced landing . Examination of the crash site by personnel from the Director of Civil Aviation Nassau Bahamas revealed one fuel tank was full of fuel and the other fuel tank was empty .
