0. At V1 the DC-10-30's number 1 engine a General Electric CF6-50C2 experienced a casing breach when the 2nd-stage low pressure turbine (LPT) anti-rotation nozzle locks failed . The change required modification of the LPT case nozzle lock holes . The nozzle segments had rotated 120 degrees within the LPT case but the case itself was not breached .
