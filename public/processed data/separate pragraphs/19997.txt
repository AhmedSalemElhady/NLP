0. On December 26 2001 about 1900 eastern standard time a Cessna 182 N660DD experienced a total loss of engine power and was substantially damage during a forced landing near Falmouth Kentucky . The pilot was unable to restart the engine and performed a forced landing to a field . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed for the flight that departed Bowman Field (LOU) Louisville Kentucky; destined for Bolton Field (TZR) Columbus Ohio .
