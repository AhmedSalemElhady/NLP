On December 18 2001 approximately 1045 mountain standard time a Piper PA-28-235 N8704W operated by Cherokee Flyers LLC of Grand Junction Colorado was substantially damaged when it collided with terrain during landing at Crested Butte Colorado . The private pilot and two passengers were not injured . The pilot applied right rudder but to no avail The airplane collided with a snow bank on the south side of the runway and the nose landing gear collapsed .
