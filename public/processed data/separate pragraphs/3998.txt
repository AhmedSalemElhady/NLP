On May 21 2000 at 1530 mountain standard time a Cessna 182N airplane N92755 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Nogales State of Sonora in the Republic of Mexico . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed for the cross country flight for which a VFR flight plan was filed .
