On October 24 2001 at 0315 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-28-181 N4149D collided with trees following a loss of engine power about four miles west of the Brunswick; Glynco Jetport in Brunswick Georgia . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors . The airplane was operated by the private pilot under the provisions of Title 14 CFR Part 91 and instrument flight rules (IFR) .
