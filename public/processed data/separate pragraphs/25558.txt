On September 25 1997 approximately 0939 central daylight time Trans World Airlines flight 593 a McDonald Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) airplane N931TW was not damaged during a runway overrun at New Orleans International Airport (Moisant Field) New Orleans Louisiana . The aircraft was being operated by as a scheduled domestic passenger flight under the provisions of Title 14 CFR Part 121 . According to ATC tower personnel the light rain had ceased prior to the arrival of flight 593 and the runway condition was classified as wet with good braking action .
