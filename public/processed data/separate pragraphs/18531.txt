On November 16 2001 at about 0154 central standard time a Cessna 152 N89645 registered to an individual collided with trees and crashed on the north bank of the Wolf River near Poplarville Mississippi while on a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight from Ocean Springs Mississippi to Brookhaven Mississippi . The flight originated from Ocean Springs on November 16 2001 about 0100 .
