On September 25 1997 approximately 0939 central daylight time Trans World Airlines flight 593 a McDonald Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) airplane N931TW was not damaged during a runway overrun at New Orleans International Airport (Moisant Field) New Orleans Louisiana . According to preliminary information the aircraft landed on runway 01 and during the landing roll the aircraft overran the departure end of the runway resulting in the nose landing gear getting stuck in the mud . The passengers were deplaned and transported to the terminal by bus .
