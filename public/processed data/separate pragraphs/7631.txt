On September 7 2000 about 1320 Greenwich Mean Time a McDonnell-Douglas DC-10-30 N15069 operating as Continental Airlines flight 71 received minor damage when an uncontained engine failure occurred during a takeoff roll at Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (HAM) Schiphol The Netherlands . An instrument flight rules flight plan had been filed for the flight between Amsterdam and Newark International Airport (EWR) Newark New Jersey .
