On December 14 1995 about 0014 central standard time N503U an Aero Commander 1121 operated by American Air Network crashed about 10 miles North of Guatemala City Guatemala while on a 14 CFR Part 135 on-demand international cargo flight . The crew executed three instrument approaches to the Guatemala City Airport and reported to controllers on duty that they were low on fuel and could not proceed to their alternate airport .
