On April 9 2001 about 1208 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-46-500TP N262MM registered to LK Aero Corporation collided with trees shortly after takeoff from Vero Beach Municipal Airport Vero Beach Florida while on a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight to Daytona Beach Florida . Recorded communications from the FAA Vero Beach Air Traffic Control Tower showed that about 30 seconds after being cleared for takeoff the pilot called and stated he needed to return to the airport and land .
