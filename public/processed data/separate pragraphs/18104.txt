On November 7 2001 approximately 2000 central standard time a Taylorcraft F19 N20002 piloted by a private pilot was substantially damaged during cruise flight when it struck a tree and subsequently impacted into a wooded area 9 miles west of Backus Minnesota . The cross-country flight originated at Park Rapids Minnesota approximately 1940 and was en route to Pine River Minnesota . The right horizontal stabilizer and elevator were bent and broken aft .
