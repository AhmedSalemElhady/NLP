0. On July 22 2001 about 1510 eastern daylight time a Beech BE-36 N3680U was substantially damaged during takeoff at the Biddeford Municipal Airport (B19) Biddeford Maine . The airplane continued down the runway until it departed the end of the runway and impacted several small trees . According to a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) inspector when the airplane reached rotation speed the pilot was not able to pull-back on the control column .
