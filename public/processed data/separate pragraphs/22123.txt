Two work orders had been signed off on the day of the accident; one indicated that the airplane was in an airworthy; the other (with a later invoice number) indicated that the airplane was not in an airworthy condition . When queried after takeoff the pilot informed ATC that the left engine was not developing full power and that he was having difficulty in maintaining altitude . Examination of the left and right engine did not reveal any condition that would have caused an operational problem .
