On July 2 2000 about 2052 central daylight time a Gulfstream American AA5A N26937 registered to an individual made a forced landing in trees near Selma Alabama following loss of engine power while on a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight . He could not make the road and elected to land the aircraft in trees . As he approached the airport he realized he would not make it to the runway and turned toward a road for a landing .
