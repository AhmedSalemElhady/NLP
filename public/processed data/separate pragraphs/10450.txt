On February 15 2001 about 1540 hours Pacific standard time a midair collision occurred between a Cessna 152 N68763 and a Cessna 172N N4686G over the Pacific Ocean about 6 nautical miles south of the Long Beach Municipal Airport Long Beach California . The Cessna 152 departed from the Long Beach airport about 1513 and the Cessna 172 departed from the Long Beach airport about 1500 . In each airplane a Long Beach Flying Club certified flight instructor and a student sustained fatal injuries .
