Postcrash examination of the wreckage revealed the left wing leading edge was found crushed aft to the spar at the midpoint and the left wing spray boom was dented/bent approximately 25 feet inboard from the outboard end . The pilot reported that he entered the field and felt a 'hard right hand pressure that he could not correct' . A witness in the first field reported that she heard a loud 'pop' and she looked up to see the airplane flying southeast across the field .
