The aircraft was leased from Winemiller Aviation by Gulf and Caribbean Cargo Inc who were operating the aircraft as a 14 CFR Part 125 nonscheduled passenger flight for Metro Nord Travel Club Cap Haitien Haiti . After extension of the wing flaps and landing gear the aircraft lost hydraulic pressure . On March 18 1995 about 1321 eastern standard time a Convair 440 N137CA registered to Winemiller Aviation collided with a parked U S Army UH-60 Blackhawk helicopter while landing at Cap Haitien Haiti while on a 14 CFR Part 125 nonscheduled passenger flight .
