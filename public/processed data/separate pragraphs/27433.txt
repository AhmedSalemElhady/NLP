On February 3 1998 at about 1200 eastern standard time a Douglas DC3C N200MF registered to Missionary Flights International as a 14 CFR Part 91 passenger flight experienced a total loss of engine power on both engines and made a forced landing 1 mile from the Greater Exuma Airport Moss Town Bahamas . The PIC stated to company personnel that he experienced an engine failure on the left engine while en route to Cape Haitian Haiti . The investigation is under the jurisdiction of the government of the Bahamas .
