On October 10 2000 about 2230 greenwich mean time (GMT) a McDonnell Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) N963TW operated as Trans World Airlines flight number 920 encountered in-flight pitch oscillations during the flight's descent prior to landing at Las Americas International Airport Santo Domingo Dominican Republic (SDQ) . The 14 CFR Part 121 scheduled international passenger flight departed from Luis Munoz Marin International Airport near San Juan Puerto Rico at 2203 GMT and landed at SDQ at 2243 GMT .
