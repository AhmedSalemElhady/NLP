On December 11 2000 at 1445 local time (1845 GMT) a Cessna U206G single-engine airplane Bolivian registration CP-1721 was destroyed upon collision with a tree and terrain while landing at a private airstrip near Sumacal Department of Beni in the Republic of Bolivia . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . The business flight originated from Trinidad at 1245 local .
