On September 13 1999 about 2045 Universal Coordinated Time a Kaman Aerospace K1200 K-MAX helicopter registration D-HFZA operated by Heli Air Zagel of Hersbruck Germany impacted terrain during a forced landing in Austria and was destroyed . During the landing the helicopter rolled onto its side and the engine could still be heard running at a low RPM .
