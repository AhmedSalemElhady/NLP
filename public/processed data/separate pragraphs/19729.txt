On December 18 2001 approximately 1045 mountain standard time a Piper PA-28-235 N8704W operated by Cherokee Flyers LLC of Grand Junction Colorado was substantially damaged when it collided with terrain during landing at Crested Butte Colorado . According to the pilot he landed on runway 29 and the airplane began veering off to the left . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed .
