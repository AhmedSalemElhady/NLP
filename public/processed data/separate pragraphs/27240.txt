On January 1 1998 about 1530 Atlantic standard time a Boeing 757-200 G-WJAN operated by Airtours Inc of the United Kingdom as a 14 CFR Part 129 charter flight impacted the ground with the tail of the airplane while landing near Puerto Plata Dominican Republic . Two flight crew members six flight attendants and 211 passengers were not injured . The airplane's tail sustained damage .
