Inspection of the airplane revealed that the leading edge of the left wing had an impact mark with a rust-colored braided appearance resembling the skyline wire there was also a similar scrape on the lower spar cap of the left wing that had remained attached to the airplane . About 30 minutes later an ALNOT was issued and the wreckage was located the following morning . A circular gouge was observed on the leading edge of one of the propeller blades; the diameter of the gouge corresponded to the end of the severed wire .
