On January 22 2000 about 1430 hours Pacific standard time a Mince Jurka MJ77 N751JR sustained substantial damage during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Rio Vista California . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . The pilot told the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) accident coordinator the airplane was a 3/4 scale P-51 with a fuel injected automotive engine .
