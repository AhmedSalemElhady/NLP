On October 2 1999 at 1615 GMT a Bell 214 ST twin-engine helicopter N704H was substantially damaged upon impact with the water during takeoff from an offshore platform in the Arabian Gulf near Dhahran in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia . The investigation is under the jurisdiction and control of the Government of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia .
