0. On January 17 2001 at 1010 Universal Coordinated Time (UTC) a Beech 95-D55 registration D-ILPY operated by Deutsche Verkehrsfliegerschule Hamburg was destroyed following impact with terrain . The airplane entered a spiral and impacted in a meadow off the departure end of the takeoff runway . Two persons were on board the airplane serving as a flightcrew of two pilots and both sustained serious injuries .
