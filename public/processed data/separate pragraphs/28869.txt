The student's flight instructor told the Safety Board that the student who had 136 hours of dual instruction and 20 hours of solo flight time had received instruction in wake turbulence avoidance at three points in ground instruction . The report concluded that the Cessna 152 descended into the Boeing 757's wake when the wake was 37 to 41 seconds old . Witnesses reported that as the student pilot was on final approach descending through approximately 100-foot altitude (agl) the aircraft abruptly rolled inverted and crashed short of the runway threshold .
