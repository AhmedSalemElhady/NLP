On November 17 1995 at approximately 1700 central standard time a Piper PA-23-160 N3283P was substantially damaged during a forced landing near Tipejuanes Mexico . The pilot further stated that he visually checked the quality of the fuel after he refueled the airplane at Nuevo Laredo Mexico .
