On August 26 2001 at 0954 eastern daylight time a Piper PA-32-300 N2116G collided with power lines and a tree while executed an emergency off-airport landing in Homerville Georgia after experiencing a loss of engine power in flight . The airplane struck power lines and a tree and came to rest near a housing area and a prison . When this was not successful in restoring adequate engine power the pilot contacted Valdosta Approach and declared an emergency .
