On October 16 2001 about 2030 eastern daylight time a Cessna 172L N811P was substantially damaged while landing at the Northampton Airport (7B2) Northampton Massachusetts . The pilot performed an approach to runway 14 a 3506-foot long 50-foot wide asphalt runway; however the airplane landed hard and sustained substantial damage to the landing gear wings and engine .
