0. On Tuesday August 8 2000 at approximately 3:44pm Air Tran flight 913 a Boeing (Douglas) DC-9 returned to its departure point Greensboro NC shortly after takeoff . Four crew members received minor injuries from smoke inhalation inflight and one passenger received a minor injury during the evacuation; one crewmember and 57 passengers were uninjured . According to information obtained from the Safety Board's interviews with the flight crew the captain and first officer noticed a smell of smoke shortly after takeoff .
