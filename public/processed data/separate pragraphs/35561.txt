Sound spectrum analysis of an FAA re-recording of communications between the pilot and ATC indicated there were electronic signatures present attributable to propeller noise at cruise setting for that type of aircraft . At that time the aircraft was about 9 miles southwest of Bimini Bahamas on a southeasterly heading at a speed of about 200 knots and an altitude of about 21000 feet . Rescue personnel located debris and fuel at latitude 25 degrees 470 minutes north longitude 079 degrees 236 minutes west consistent with the position relayed by ARTCC .
