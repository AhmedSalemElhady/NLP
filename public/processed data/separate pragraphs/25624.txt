On September 25 1997 approximately 0939 central daylight time Trans World Airlines flight 593 a McDonald Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) airplane N931TW was not damaged during a runway overrun at New Orleans International Airport (Moisant Field) New Orleans Louisiana . The flight crew reported to ATC that the breaking action was good during the beginning of the landing roll; however during the last portion of the landing roll the breaking action became poor . According to preliminary information the aircraft landed on runway 01 and during the landing roll the aircraft overran the departure end of the runway resulting in the nose landing gear getting stuck in the mud .
