0. On August 14 2000 about 1252 hours Pacific daylight time a Beech K35 N878R owned and operated by the pilot experienced an in-flight emergency during cruise flight . On final approach to the westbound traffic lanes the airplane collided with three automobiles . Oil was observed on components including the face (rearward surface) of the propeller blades the exposed portion of the crankshaft upper and lower engine cowl the front windscreen fuselage belly and the empennage .
