On October 10 2000 about 2230 greenwich mean time (GMT) a McDonnell Douglas DC-9-83 (MD-83) N963TW operated as Trans World Airlines flight number 920 encountered in-flight pitch oscillations during the flight's descent prior to landing at Las Americas International Airport Santo Domingo Dominican Republic (SDQ) . This is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors . The 6 crewmembers and 19 passengers were uninjured .
