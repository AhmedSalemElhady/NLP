On August 22 2001 about 2100 Alaska daylight time a tundra tire equipped Piper PA-12 airplane N7604H sustained substantial damage during landing at an off airport site about 18 miles east of Nenana Alaska . The solo certificated private pilot was not injured . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) personal flight under Title 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred .
