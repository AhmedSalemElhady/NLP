On March 18 1995 about 1321 eastern standard time a Convair 440 N137CA registered to Winemiller Aviation collided with a parked U S Army UH-60 Blackhawk helicopter while landing at Cap Haitien Haiti while on a 14 CFR Part 125 nonscheduled passenger flight . The approach was continued and the emergency air brake was armed .
