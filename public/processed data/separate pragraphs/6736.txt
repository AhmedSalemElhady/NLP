On August 14 2000 about 1252 hours Pacific daylight time a Beech K35 N878R owned and operated by the pilot experienced an in-flight emergency during cruise flight . Initial reports indicated that one of the occupants in an automobile was seriously injured and another occupant in the same vehicle received minor injuries .
