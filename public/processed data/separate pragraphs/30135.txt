1. The pilot had filed an instrument flight rules (IFR) flight plan for 25000 feet mean sea level (MSL) and he amended it to 27000 feet MSL en route . About 36 minutes after the altitude change to 27000 feet the pilot advised air traffic control (ATC) that he had lost cabin pressurization and needed an immediate descent .
