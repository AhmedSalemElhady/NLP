The aft bearing (gear case cover) of the FCU/oil pump drive spur gear had disintegrated due to failure of the ribbon-type ball separator which was found in the bottom of the gearbox in two pieces and deformed . Compliance was due at the next accessory gearbox repair when the gearbox was opened . Allison CEB 1228 issued November 20 1985 calls for bearings with the ribbon-type pressed steel separator to be replaced with bearings that incorporate a one-piece machined steel separator .
