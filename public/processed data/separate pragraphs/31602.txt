On December 6 1998 about 0915 eastern standard time a Piper PA-38-112 N9471T registered to Bug Air Transport Co operating as a 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight crashed into a salt marsh 6 miles west of Marsh Harbor International Airport Abaco Bahamas following a sudden engine stoppage . The flight originated from Hollywood/Fort Lauderdale International Airport at about 0720 . The airplane nosed over upon impact with the marsh .
