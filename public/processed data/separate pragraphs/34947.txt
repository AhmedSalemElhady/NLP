Inspection of the airplane revealed that the leading edge of the left wing had an impact mark with a rust-colored braided appearance resembling the skyline wire there was also a similar scrape on the lower spar cap of the left wing that had remained attached to the airplane . At the center of the valley the wires are approximately horizontal and are suspended approximately 175 feet above the valley floor .
