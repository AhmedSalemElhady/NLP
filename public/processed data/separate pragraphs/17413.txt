On October 20 2001 at 1250 eastern daylight time a North American SNJ-6 N453WA collided on the ground with a Cessna CE-172-R N64AF after landing at Kissimmee Municipal Airport in Kissimmee Florida . The pilot of the SNJ-6 then lost sight of the Cessna and the SNJ-6 propeller subsequently collided with the tail of the Cessna . The sightseeing flight of the SNJ-6 was operated under the provisions of Title 14 CFR Part 91 with no flight plan on file .
