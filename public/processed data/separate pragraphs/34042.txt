The flight operating as AVIATECA Flight 901 (GU901) was on an IFR flight plan from La Aurora International Airport Guatamala City Guatemala to San Salvador International Airport San Salvador El Salvador . Severe weather existed at the time of the 14 CFR 129 flight and the aircraft had divated from airway G436 to the north to avoid thunderstorms . The aircraft was owned by CIT Leasing Corporation 1211 Avenue of the Americas New York New York 10036 and leased to AVIATECA S A The investigation is continuing under the jurisdiction of Director General de Transporte Aereo the government of El Salvador .
