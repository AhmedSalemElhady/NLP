On August 6 2001 about 1200 Alaska daylight time a Cessna 172S airplane N813SP sustained substantial damage during an emergency landing at the Quartz Creek Airport located about 3 miles east of Cooper Landing Alaska . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) local area personal flight when the accident occurred . According to information provided by an Alaska State Trooper and information provided by a friend of the pilot on August 6th the airplane was in cruise flight after departing Anchorage .
