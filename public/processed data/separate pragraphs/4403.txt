The surviving rear seat passenger/photographer (pax) stated that during a preflight briefing the pilot indicated that they would fly by a cottage at reduced airspeed and at about 500 feet agl then they would climb to a higher altitude for more photographs . One witness stated the engine sounded as if it was sputtering; another stated that it was noisy and loud for a small airplane . The pax said the pilot did not respond but was leaning forward as if to be trying to read or reach something .
