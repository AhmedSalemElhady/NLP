On January No 13 1994 about 1230 hours Greenwich Mean Time a Beech BE-90 N46WA registered to Charles Kuykendall Wilmer Texas was destroyed during a ditching in international waters about 50 nautical miles south of Martigues France . According to the pilot an electrical fire started and produced smoke in the cockpit during the flight from Straubling-Wallmuhl Germany to the Azores Portugal . According to Hungarian records the airplane was substantially damaged in a previous accident on March 9 1991 .
