On December 13 2001 approximately 1255 mountain standard time a Cessna 172N N73892 operated by McAir Aviation was substantially damaged when it collided with terrain following a hard landing at Jeffco Airport Broomfield Colorado . According to the pilot he was making his third practice touch-and-go landing . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed and no flight plan had been filed for the personal flight being conducted under Title 14 CFR Part 91 .
