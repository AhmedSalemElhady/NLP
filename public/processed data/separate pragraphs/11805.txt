On April 14 2001 about 1830 eastern daylight time a Grumman American AA-5B N81189 registered to and operated by a private owner as a Title 14 CFR Part 91 personal flight  collided with a fence during landing at a private field in Dade City Florida . The private-rated pilot and one passenger were not injured and the airplane incurred substantial damage .
