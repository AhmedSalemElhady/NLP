On May 21 2000 at 1530 mountain standard time a Cessna 182N airplane N92755 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near Nogales State of Sonora in the Republic of Mexico . The flight's destination was the Nogales Airport (MMNG) near Nogales State of Sonora Mexico . One passenger was seriously injured and the private pilot and another passenger were uninjured .
