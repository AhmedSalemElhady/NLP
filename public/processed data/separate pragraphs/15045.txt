On August 15 2001 at approximately 1450 mountain daylight time a Cessna 172N N733BW was substantially damaged following impact with terrain in an uncontrolled descent at Meadow Lake Airport Peyton Colorado . The student was flying his third solo flight when he impacted terrain near the approach end to runway 33 . American Aviation Inc of Peyton Colorado was operating the airplane under Title 14 CFR Part 91 .
