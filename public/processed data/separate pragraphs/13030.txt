On May 27 2001 at 1255 hours Pacific daylight time a Cessna 337G N337GD sustained substantial damage when it impacted electrical power transmission wires about 5 miles south of Temecula California . The airplane's windshield was broken out doorposts were bent and wing struts were damaged . The personal cross-country flight departed Brown Field San Diego California about 1235 and was destined for the French Valley Airport Temecula .
