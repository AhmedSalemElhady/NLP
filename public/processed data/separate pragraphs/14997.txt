On August 15 2001 at approximately 1450 mountain daylight time a Cessna 172N N733BW was substantially damaged following impact with terrain in an uncontrolled descent at Meadow Lake Airport Peyton Colorado . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed for the local solo instructional flight that originated approximately 35 minutes before the accident .
