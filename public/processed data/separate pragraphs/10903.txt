On March 23 2001 about 1550 hours mountain standard time a Cessna 305A N5188G owned and operated by the front seated pilot ground looped during landing rollout on runway 04 at the Winslow-Lindbergh Regional Airport Winslow Arizona . The flight instructor (CFI) reported that the front seated student held a private pilot certificate but he had not received an endorsement to fly conventional gear airplanes .
