The Vice President of RotorWay International agreed to issue an Advisory Bulletin to all registered owners of RotorWay helicopters having the RI-162 engine informing them of the background of this accident as well as alternate way of carburetor icing prevention and avoidance . According to carburetor icing charts the accident flight was operating in conditions favorable to carburetor icing . According to the pilot/builder the homebuilt helicopter was in cruise at 1000 feet agl when he initiated a 200-300 foot per minute descent .
