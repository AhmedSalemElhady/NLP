0. On July 10 2000 at 1345 hours Pacific daylight time a Piper PA-28-180 N4209T collided with obstructions during an off airport forced landing in Santa Maria California . The aircraft was being operated by the pilot/owner as a personal flight under 14 CFR Part 91 when the accident occurred . The airplane landed near Inger and Sequoia streets about 1 mile from the airport .
