According to witnesses working several miles east of the accident site they heard an airplane flying overhead traveling east to west and low to the ground; however low cloud cover snow and poor visibility prevented them from viewing the aircraft . The pilot departed Lee's Summit Missouri on the morning of the accident to visit family members in Kremmling Colorado . The last National Track Analysis Program (NTAP) radar 'hit' of the accident airplane was at 1019:23 at an altitude of 12600 feet .
