0. On September 7 2001 about 1145 Alaska daylight time a wheel-equipped de Havilland DHC-3 airplane N254AW sustained substantial damage during a forced landing about 53 miles east of Cordova Alaska . The airplane was registered to Thomas J Prijatel and operated by Alaska Wilderness Outfitting Company LLC Cordova Alaska . During the subsequent forced landing in a marshy muskeg-covered area the airplane nosed over and sustained substantial damage to right wing strut engine firewall and empennage .
