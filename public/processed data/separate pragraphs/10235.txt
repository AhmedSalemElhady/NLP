0. On January 17 2001 at 1010 Universal Coordinated Time (UTC) a Beech 95-D55 registration D-ILPY operated by Deutsche Verkehrsfliegerschule Hamburg was destroyed following impact with terrain . Two persons were on board the airplane serving as a flightcrew of two pilots and both sustained serious injuries . The flightcrew reported that during initial climb following takeoff from Rendsburg-Schachtholm Airport an engine malfunction occurred followed by a loss of control of the airplane .
