On October 20 2001 at 1250 eastern daylight time a North American SNJ-6 N453WA collided on the ground with a Cessna CE-172-R N64AF after landing at Kissimmee Municipal Airport in Kissimmee Florida . Shortly afterward the SNJ-6 landed on runway 6 and was advised by air traffic control to follow the Cessna . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed at the time of the accident .
