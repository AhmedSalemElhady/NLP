On October 20 2001 at 1250 eastern daylight time a North American SNJ-6 N453WA collided on the ground with a Cessna CE-172-R N64AF after landing at Kissimmee Municipal Airport in Kissimmee Florida . Any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed . Shortly afterward the SNJ-6 landed on runway 6 and was advised by air traffic control to follow the Cessna .
