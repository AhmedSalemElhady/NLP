On August 6 2001 about 1200 Alaska daylight time a Cessna 172S airplane N813SP sustained substantial damage during an emergency landing at the Quartz Creek Airport located about 3 miles east of Cooper Landing Alaska . The airplane was being operated as a visual flight rules (VFR) local area personal flight when the accident occurred . The airplane was registered to AvAlaska inc Anchorage Alaska and operated by the pilot .
