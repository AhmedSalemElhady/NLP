On February 15 2001 about 1540 hours Pacific standard time a midair collision occurred between a Cessna 152 N68763 and a Cessna 172N N4686G over the Pacific Ocean about 6 nautical miles south of the Long Beach Municipal Airport Long Beach California . Both airplanes were being operated under the provisions of 14 CFR Part 91 . Witnesses reported that following the collision both airplanes entered uncontrolled descents impacted the water and sank .
