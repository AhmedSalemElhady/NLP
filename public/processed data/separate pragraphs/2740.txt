0. At V1 the DC-10-30's number 1 engine a General Electric CF6-50C2 experienced a casing breach when the 2nd-stage low pressure turbine (LPT) anti-rotation nozzle locks failed . The change required modification of the LPT case nozzle lock holes . Debris from the breach resulted in collateral damage to the numbers 2 and 3 engines the fuselage and the left landing gear .
