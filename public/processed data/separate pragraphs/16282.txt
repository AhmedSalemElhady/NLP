1. The flight originated about 1115 from the airplane owner's remote lodge located about 100 miles southeast of Cordova  and was en route to Cordova . During a telephone conversation with the National Transportation Safety Board investigator-in-charge on September 8 the airplane/lodge owner reported that while in level cruise flight the accident pilot noted a loud bang as the engine began to run rough and lose power . He said that as the pilot was performing the engine emergency procedures he smelled smoke in the cabin and noted that the oil pressure was rapidly decreasing .
