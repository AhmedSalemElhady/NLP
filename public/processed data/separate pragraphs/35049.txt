Inspection by an FAA airworthiness inspector revealed that a nose landing gear assembly from an earlier model Cessna 182 had been installed without a shimmy damper and without provision for connection to the steering bungees . The airplane had received its last annual inspection on July 18 1997 26 months before the first accident . The private pilot who was also a mechanic with an inspection authorization was landing the airplane from a ferry flight after the airplane had been involved in a previous accident (see ANC99LA155) .
