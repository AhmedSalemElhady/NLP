The aft bearing (gear case cover) of the FCU/oil pump drive spur gear had disintegrated due to failure of the ribbon-type ball separator which was found in the bottom of the gearbox in two pieces and deformed . Allison CEB 1228 issued November 20 1985 calls for bearings with the ribbon-type pressed steel separator to be replaced with bearings that incorporate a one-piece machined steel separator . The mechanic reported that he had replaced the number 25 bearing and the spur adapter gear shaft 265 hours and 4 months before the accident which would have provided access to the gearbox to accomplish the bulletin .
