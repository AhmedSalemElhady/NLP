Records obtained from the U S repair station indicated that the airplane incurred an extensive maintenance history with needed repairs including damaged wiring and leaks in the fuel system as well as structural leaks which had lead to several pressurization difficulties . At 1114:17 the pilot of YV385CP told FAA ARTCC that he was experiencing an emergency and at 1114:29 he asked for immediate directions to Bimini . At that time the aircraft was about 9 miles southwest of Bimini Bahamas on a southeasterly heading at a speed of about 200 knots and an altitude of about 21000 feet .
