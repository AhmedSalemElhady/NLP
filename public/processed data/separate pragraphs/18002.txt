On November 2 2001 about 1410 eastern standard time a Cessna 172D N36SK was substantially damaged when it impacted terrain near Argyle New York . Due to strong winds the airplane lost lift and it impacted an alfalfa field about 1/2 mile from the approach end of runway 21 .
