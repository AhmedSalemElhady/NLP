Some witnesses reported a 'popping' sound or a 'laboring' sound from the engines; however after the engines were recovered at a later date no evidence of a preimpact mechanical failure or malfunction was found . Local residents reported that the water condition at the time of the accident was 'calm' or 'glassy' . Several small pieces of the fuselage and main rotor blades were found floating on the surface of the water .
