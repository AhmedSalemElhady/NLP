On July 31 2001 at 1347 mountain daylight time a Bell 205A-1 helicopter N205BR sustained substantial damage when the water bucket of a Bell 212 helicopter N911VR contacted its main rotor system near Jackson Wyoming . Visual meteorological conditions prevailed for the local flights . The airline transport pilot of the Bell 205A-1 and the airline transport pilot of the Bell 212 were not injured .
